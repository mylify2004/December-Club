December Club

December Club is a private protocol.

It is not a platform, not a service, and not a social network.

It exists for individuals who require controlled communication, absolute discretion, and the absence of digital trace.

What December Club Is

December Club provides a secured, invitation-based environment for private conversations and closed meetings, accessible globally.

  • Members do not create rooms.
  • Members do not host freely.
  • All access is centrally managed.
  • Every meeting exists only in its moment.
  • No recordings.
  • No archives.
  • No history.

What We Offer

  • Private audio/video meeting rooms
  • Centralized access approval
